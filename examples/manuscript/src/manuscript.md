## Algorithm examples

These examples use the `algorithmic` environment (from the `algorithmcx` package:)

\begin{algorithmic}
\Require $n \geq 0$
\Ensure $y = x^n$
\State $y \Leftarrow 1$
\State $X \Leftarrow x$
\State $N \Leftarrow n$
\While{$N \neq 0$}
\If{$N$ is even}
  \State $X \Leftarrow X \times X$
  \State $N \Leftarrow \frac{N}{2} $  \Comment{This is a comment}
\ElsIf{$N$ is odd}
  \State $y \Leftarrow y \times X$
  \State $N \Leftarrow N - 1$
\EndIf
\EndWhile
\end{algorithmic}


\begin{algorithmic}[1]
\Repeat
\Comment{forever}
\State this\Until{you die.}
\end{algorithmic}
